About Us

Hotel Chaoune - An Oasis of Serenity in Arzew

Located in the vibrant coastal city of Arzew, the 3-star Hotel Chaoune (⭐⭐⭐) offers a unique retreat that blends modern comfort with a traditional touch. Inspired by beautiful architecture and calming blue color palettes, our hotel is designed to provide you with a peaceful, relaxing environment.

Whether you are visiting for business or a family holiday, our prime location near the coastal mountains and pristine beaches, combined with our exceptional service, ensures a memorable stay. We take pride in delivering the highest standards of authentic Algerian hospitality.
